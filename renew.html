Thumbprint Renew

Your Best Content, Reborn.

Content modernization, LMS hosting & fractional CLO.

We take your existing training programs, curricula, and onboarding materials and transform them into modern, multi-modal experiences that actually engage learners.

What Renew Delivers

Three Service Tracks

Each engagement is custom-scoped after a discovery call. Pricing is based on the size, complexity, and scope of your existing content.

Track 1

Content Modernization

  • ✓Full audit of existing curriculum, training & onboarding materials
  • ✓Multi-modal redesign — video, interactive, scenario-based, micro-learning
  • ✓Engagement upgrade — storytelling, gamification, real-world application
  • ✓Brand alignment — updated visuals, voice, and delivery

Pricing: Project-based

Track 2

LMS Hosting & Administration

  • ✓Platform selection, setup, or migration
  • ✓Ongoing fractional LMS admin — user management, reporting, updates
  • ✓Learner experience design — pathways, progression, completion tracking

Pricing: Monthly retainer

Track 3

Fractional CLO Services

  • ✓Strategic learning roadmap aligned to business goals
  • ✓Content calendar and curriculum planning
  • ✓Learning outcome measurement and reporting

Pricing: Monthly retainer (hours-based)
