MagnaCon 2026 — Workshop Session

Build the Brand Behind
Your Practice

Todd's MagnaCon session broke down why most MagnaWave practitioners stay invisible online — and what to do about it. Here are the key insights, AI tools he referenced, and a starter checklist to take action this week.

June 10–11 MagnaCon 2026
Presenter Todd Schaeffer
Topic Digital Presence & AI Content

Session Highlights

The Core Ideas from Todd's Talk

If you've been relying on word-of-mouth alone, you're leaving real growth on the table. Todd walked through the gap most practitioners never close — and how AI tools make it possible to show up consistently without adding 10 hours to your week.

01
Your Story Is the Strategy
The most effective MagnaWave content isn't about the machine — it's about transformation. Real client stories, your own journey, and the "why" behind your practice are what stop the scroll.
02
Consistency Beats Perfection
You don't need a production crew. A system that produces even two strong posts a week — every week — will outperform a perfectly produced video that goes up twice a year.
03
One Ideal Client, One Message
Practitioners who try to reach everyone reach no one. Defining your ideal client — athletic recovery, chronic pain, equine — and speaking only to them is what turns followers into bookings.
04
AI Is a Leverage Tool, Not a Replacement
The practitioners growing fastest aren't writing more — they're writing smarter. AI tools handle first drafts, captions, and repurposing so your energy goes into relationships and sessions.
05
Pick One Platform and Own It
Spreading across five platforms dilutes everything. Pick the one where your client actually lives — Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, or TikTok — and build there first before expanding.
06
Every Post Needs a Next Step
Engagement without direction is wasted attention. Every piece of content — whether educational or personal — should guide the reader toward one clear action: book, learn more, DM you.

"MagnaWave works. You've seen it. Your clients feel it. The only thing standing between where you are and a full schedule is whether the right people can find you — and whether they trust you before they ever meet you."

Todd Schaeffer — MagnaCon 2026

Action Plan

Your First-Week Checklist

Todd's closing challenge: don't leave MagnaCon and do nothing. Pick five items from this list and complete them before next Monday.

Week-One Action Checklist
Complete these before your next session day. Each item takes 30 minutes or less.
  • Write one sentence that describes who you help and how — your positioning statement.
  • Pick your primary platform (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, or TikTok) and commit to it for 90 days.
  • Draft three content ideas using ChatGPT: one educational, one story-based, one client result.
  • Record a 60-second video answering "What is MagnaWave and who is it for?" — no editing required.
  • Set up a free scheduling tool (Buffer or Later) and schedule your first three posts.
  • Audit your bio on every platform — does it say who you help and what to do next?
  • Add one clear CTA to your existing posts or profile (link to booking, DM to inquire, etc.).
  • Identify your ideal client in one sentence — niche beats broad, every time.

Content Ideas

30 Days of MagnaWave Content Starters

Never stare at a blank caption again. Run each of these through ChatGPT or Claude with your voice and client focus — you'll have a month of content in an afternoon.

Educational
Myth vs. Fact Posts
"3 things people get wrong about PEMF therapy." Correcting misconceptions builds trust faster than any promotion.
Story
Behind the Session
What does a typical MagnaWave appointment actually look like? Walk your audience through it — the setup, the machine, the results.
Results
Before & After Moments
Client testimonials, movement improvements, animal behavior changes. Show the work — with permission. Results convert.
FAQ
Answer Real Questions
"Is it safe for my horse?" "How many sessions does it take?" Use actual questions you get in consultations as content prompts.
Personal
Why You Started
Your origin story is your most powerful differentiator. Practitioners who share their "why" consistently outperform those who don't.
Community
Share What You're Learning
Attending MagnaCon? Sharing a tip from Todd's session? Posts like this build authority and show you're invested in your practice.
